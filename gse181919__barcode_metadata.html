Off-by-one header — per-cell annotations can be silently mislabeled
(all columns)
8 header names but 9 data columns (the leading cell-barcode column is unnamed — a classic R write.table rownames export). A naive read.table(header=TRUE) shifts every header left: Age becomes an expression metric, Sample-ID becomes cluster labels, and cell.type falls off the end.
Risk: Whole 54k-cell table mislabeled with no error. Reader-conditional: pandas' default recovers it (promotes the unnamed column to index); R / index-less readers do not.
Critical
Automated profiling + row-level audit of public NCBI GEO supplementary metadata, with headline findings re-confirmed by a tool-based verifier. Findings are data-quality hypotheses, not assertions about any downstream publication; impact statements are risks. Severity reflects downstream impact.
